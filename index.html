What is Cureosity?

Cureosity is a medical guidance app designed to help elderly cancer patients and their caregivers better understand the cancer journey. We simplify complex medical information into clear, easy-to-understand explanations—so no one feels lost or overwhelmed.

Who It’s For

  • Elderly individuals navigating a cancer diagnosis
  • Caregivers and family members supporting loved ones
  • Users who need accessible, straightforward medical explanations

Key Features

  • Simple explanations of cancer treatments and recovery
  • Elder-friendly design with clear language
  • Text-to-speech support for accessibility
  • Daily check-ins and supportive messages
  • Tools to help caregivers stay informed

Important Notice

Cureosity is an educational and supportive resource. It does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment recommendations, or replace professional medical advice.
